# What Is IPTV?

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers television content over the internet instead of traditional broadcast methods. This allows users to stream live TV channels, movies, series, sports, and other content on multiple devices.

Unlike cable TV, IPTV gives users more flexibility and supports streaming on Smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, streaming boxes, and computers.

---

# How IPTV Works

An IPTV provider delivers content through internet servers.

Your IPTV player connects using:

- M3U Playlist
- Xtream Codes API
- Portal URL

The player loads live channels, video on demand, and TV guides directly from the IPTV server.

# Frequently Asked Questions

### Is IPTV legal?

Legality depends on the content source and local laws. Users should ensure they access content through authorized services.

### Does IPTV require fast internet?

For HD streaming, a stable broadband connection is recommended. Higher resolutions such as 4K generally require faster speeds.

### Which IPTV player is the best?

Popular options include TiviMate and IPTV Smarters Pro depending on your device and preferences.
